Quick Pay on-the-go

Users must register with their bank account details, which can be done through their bank's mobile app or website. To initiate a transaction using FINTECH, the user enters the amount they wish to send and selects the recipient's ID or mobile number. The user then enters their PIN to authenticate the transaction. Once the transaction is authenticated, the funds are transferred instantly from the sender's bank account to the recipient's bank account. Both the sender and recipient receive confirmation of the transaction, along with details such as the transaction ID and the amount transferred. One of the key advantages is that they enable users to make payments directly from their bank accounts, eliminating the need for a separate wallet or card. Additionally, transactions are processed in real-time, making it a fast and convenient way to transfer money.

Customized Budgeting

Customized budgeting is a valuable feature of modern financial trackers that can help users gain a deeper understanding of their financial situation and track progress towards specific financial goals. Personalized budget categories allow users to create custom budget categories based on their unique spending patterns and financial goals.Users can create separate categories for groceries, entertainment, or loan payments. FINTECH uses machine learning algorithms to automatically categorize expenses, making it easier for users to track their spending and identify areas where they can cut back. FINTECH uses predictive algorithms to forecast future expenses based on past spending patterns, allowing users to plan ahead and make adjustments to their budgets as needed. It also allows users to generate customized reports that provide a detailed view of their financial situation, including income, expenses, and savings.

Statistical Data Representation

Graphical representation used to show how expenses have changed over the course of a month. Select a time frame for which you want to track expenses. FINTECH collects data and tracks your expenses for the selected time frame. The graph shows how your expenses have changed over time. Data points are added to the graph to show how your expenses have changed over time. Each data point represents the total amount spent on expenses during a specific time period. Analyze the graph to identify patterns in your expenses. Are there specific categories of expenses that are driving up your overall spending? By creating a line graph that tracks expenses over time, you can gain valuable insights into your spending patterns and make informed decisions about how to manage your finances.
