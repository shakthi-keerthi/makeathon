Key features we have to offer.

Quick Pay

Users must register with their bank account details, which can be done through their bank's mobile app or website. To initiate a transaction using FINTECH, the user enters the amount they wish to send and selects the recipient's ID or mobile number. Additionally, transactions are processed in real-time.

Customized Budgeting

Personalized budget categories allow users to create custom budget categories based on their unique spending patterns and financial goals. FINTECH forecasts future expenses based on past spending patterns, allowing users to plan ahead & make adjustments to their budgets as needed.

Statistical Data Representation

Graphical representation is used to show how the expenses have changed over the course of a month. FINTECH collects data and tracks your expenses for the selected time frame. The graph shows how your expenses have changed over time.
